Unit Converter

As an excuse to continue learning AngularJs, I built a Unit Converter. For now it converts salaries and wages between hourly, daily, weekly, yearly etc. I wanted to focus on keeping the interface simple by hiding all options within a natural sentence, and removing options when they get in the way.
